Spazio Sassetti operates as an artist residency program in the heart of Florence, offering artists one month residencies with the culmination of a two week presentation, showcasing the work they have produced in this time. All residencies will be conducted one at a time.

Location

Spazio Sassetti is located at via dei Sassetti n.1 at the second floor of Palazzo dello Strozzino, a Renaissance palace in the historical center of Florence, Italy. The stone Renaissance facade is located on Piazza degli Strozzi, adjaent to the Southeast corner of the imposing Palazzo Strozzi. The Northern façade on Via dei Anselmi houses the entrance to the Odeon Cinema, one of earliest and most beautiful movie theaters in all of Italy. The palace is surrounded by lively shops, bars, restaurants and cafes and is just a short walk away from most of the city’s masterpieces of renaissance art and architecture.
