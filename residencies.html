Project 2 Louisa Gagliardi 04–02–2025

Louisa Gagliardi is in residence at Spazio Sassetti until May 10th.


Louisa Gagliardi (b. 1989, Sion, Switzerland) lives and works in Zurich. Gagliardi received her BA in Graphic Design from ECAL (École cantonale d'art de Lausanne) in 2012. Working at the intersection of digital and tactile processes, she creates psychologically charged tableaus that are first rendered digitally, then printed on PVC and finished with hand-applied gel and varnish. Gagliardi’s work frequently explores themes of solitude, desire, and dislocation, capturing contemporary anxieties through flattened, glossy figuration.


Recent solo exhibitions include Galerie Eva Presenhuber, Zurich (2024, 2021); MASI Lugano (2023); and the Swiss Institute, New York (2019). Group exhibitions include the Centre d’Art Contemporain Genève (2023); National Gallery Prague (2021); and Kunsthalle St. Gallen (2020). Her work is included in the collections of MASI Lugano; the Aïshti Foundation, Beirut; and the X Museum, Beijing.

Project 1 Sedrick Chisom 02–17–2025

On February 17, 2025, Spazio Sassetti welcomed its first artist-in-residence, Sedrick Chisom.


Sedrick Chisom (b. 1989, Philadelphia, PA) leans into his own myth-making and historically-bound proclivities. As such, his paintings are caught between a recuperated past and apocalyptic future, all of which is grounded in a stage play written by the artist. He habitually borrows from this plot in order to arrive at images that are both haunted and mystic in nature. Chisom lives and works in London.


Recent solo exhibitions have been held at Pilar Corrias, London (2024); CLEARING, New York (2024); Pilar Corrias, London (2021); and Matthew Brown, Los Angeles (2019). Group exhibitions include Frye Art Museum, Seattle (2022); Hayward Gallery, London (2022) and Kunsthal Rotterdam (2022–2023); and Possédées, MO.CO. Montpellier (2020).
